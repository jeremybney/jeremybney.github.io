Don't Agonize - Organize!

3D gets its inspiration from LiveStrong bracelets, from the social network nature of Venmo, and from activists around the country who are searching for a better tomorrow.

How it works

DESIGN Using a Monoprice Select 3D Printer, we custom build all of the pins for our advocates to wear. The images that we develop are all reclaimed prints from some of the most popular buttons worn in America in the 1960s.

DONATE We have created a unique Venmo account that makes donating easier than ever before. Simply text $30 to @3D-xyz and indicate your address and the organization of your choice. We will then proceed to build your pin and mail it to your house.

DEMONSTRATE 80 million LiveStrong bracelets have been sold. We hope that our friends will wear the pins that we ship them in order to increase awareness about our causes and to attract others to join the fight. In addition, after every 10 donations, we send a hand-written letter to the NY Congress Representative.
